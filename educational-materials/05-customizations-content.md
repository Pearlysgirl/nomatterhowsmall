# Module 5: Fun Customizations Content Specification

## Overview
6-slide module covering design changes, color modifications, font updates, new sections, and creative expression guidance.

## Slide 1: Making Your Website Uniquely Yours! 🎨
**Content:** Welcome to creative expression, types of customizations (colors, fonts, layouts, new sections), framing as making website reflect God's unique design for you, encouragement about creativity as gift from God.

## Slide 2: Asking Claude for Design Changes 💬
**Content:** How to communicate design desires effectively, examples of good requests ("Make the title purple and bigger", "Add a section for Bible verses"), understanding that Claude is creative partner, sample conversations showing design process.

## Slide 3: Changing Colors and Fonts ✨
**Content:** How to ask for color changes (specific colors vs mood descriptions), font modification requests, asking Claude to suggest color schemes, understanding that changes are reversible, examples of Christian color symbolism.

## Slide 4: Adding New Sections and Pages 📝
**Content:** Types of new content to add (testimony pages, prayer requests, Bible study notes), how to ask Claude to create new sections, understanding website structure, planning content before building.

## Slide 5: When to Experiment vs Ask for Help ⚖️
**Content:** Decision matrix for creative choices, when it's safe to try things yourself, when to ask Claude first, understanding reversible vs permanent changes, building confidence in creative decision-making.

## Slide 6: Creative Master Celebration! 🎉
**Content:** New creative powers, website as expression of God's creativity through you, Isaiah 64:8 about God as potter, ready for troubleshooting module.
**Achievement:** 🏆 Website Creative Master
