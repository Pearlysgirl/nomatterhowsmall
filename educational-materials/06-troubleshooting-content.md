# Module 6: Help & Troubleshooting Content Specification

## Overview
5-slide module covering problem diagnosis, Claude assistance, escalation procedures, and confidence building.

## Slide 1: Getting Help When You Need It 🆘
**Content:** Normalizing that everyone needs help sometimes, different types of help available (self-help, Claude, human support), building confidence in problem-solving, Bible verse about asking for wisdom (James 1:5).

## Slide 2: The 'My Changes Aren't Showing' Detective Guide 🔍
**Visual:** Decision tree flowchart for most common problem
**Content:** Step-by-step diagnosis process - wait 5 minutes, refresh browser, clear cache, check different device, ask Claude, contact human help. Each step with clear instructions.

## Slide 3: How to Ask Claude Effective Questions 💡
**Content:** Examples of good vs bad questions, being specific about what you want, including screenshots when helpful, asking follow-up questions, understanding Claude wants to help you succeed.

## Slide 4: When to Ask Humans for Help 👥
**Content:** Escalation path from self-help to Claude to human support, types of problems that need human help, how to describe problems clearly, understanding that asking for help is wisdom not weakness.

## Slide 5: Confident Problem-Solver Celebration! 🎉
**Content:** Problem-solving skills developed, confidence in facing challenges, Philippians 4:13 about doing all things through Christ, ready to manage website independently with support available.
**Achievement:** 🏆 Website Problem-Solving Master
