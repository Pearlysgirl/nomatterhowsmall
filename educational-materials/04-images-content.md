# Module 4: Images & Visual Updates Content Specification

## Overview
8-slide module covering photo preparation, adding images, changing hero images, creating galleries, and best practices.

## Slide 1: Adding Photos to Your Website! 📸
**Content:** Welcome to visual storytelling, types of photos to add (personal, inspirational, events, Christian symbols), Bible verse James 1:17 about good gifts from God, safety reminder about photo permissions.

## Slide 2: Where Images Live in Your Website 🏠
**Visual:** Folder structure diagram showing src/assets/images/ location
**Content:** Photo organization rules, naming conventions (no spaces, descriptive), file size limits (under 2MB), format guidelines (JPG for photos, PNG for graphics).

## Slide 3: Preparing Your Photos for the Web ✨
**Content:** Photo preparation checklist - resize to under 2MB, choose right format, use web-friendly names. Quick resize instructions for Mac Preview. Option to ask Claude for help with photo prep.

## Slide 4: Adding a New Photo Step-by-Step 📥
**Content:** Complete process - put photo in images folder, tell Claude what you want ("Add sunset-photo.jpg to inspiration section"), Claude shows changes, approve, commit and push. Example conversation included.

## Slide 5: Changing the Hero Image 🌟
**Content:** What hero image is (main banner), how to choose good hero photos (meaningful, high quality, landscape format), process for asking Claude to change it, tips for photos that inspire and welcome.

## Slide 6: Creating a Simple Photo Gallery 🖼️
**Content:** When to create galleries (events, projects, storytelling), how to organize gallery photos in subfolders, asking Claude to create galleries, choosing style options (rows, captions, clickable).

## Slide 7: Image Tips and Best Practices 💡
**Content:** Technical best practices (speed, sizing, quality), content and legal tips (photo sources, permissions, Christian considerations), asking Claude for help with image decisions.

## Slide 8: Visual Master Celebration! 🎉
**Content:** New photo powers achieved, real-world applications, Psalm 19:1 about heavens declaring God's glory, ready for next modules.
**Achievement:** 🏆 Website Visual Master
